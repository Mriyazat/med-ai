<h3 style="font-size: 1.3em; font-weight: bold; font-style: italic; color: #111827; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-top: 2em;">Challenge Overview</h3>

<p style="font-size: 1.05em; color: #334155; margin-bottom: 1.5em; font-style: italic; line-height: 1.8;">
<a href="https://github.com/ai4castinghub/hospitalization-forecast" style="color: #2563eb; font-weight: 500; text-decoration: none;">AI4Casting Hub</a> is organizing a collaborative nowcasting and forecasting challenge for confirmed hospital bed occupancy during the 2025-2026 influenza season, starting on <strong>November 20, 2025</strong>. The challenge will run until <strong>May 16, 2026</strong>. During this period, participating teams are asked to provide weekly probabilistic interval hindcasts, nowcasts and forecasts for hospital bed occupancy at both the provincial level (Ontario) and for each of the 6 Public Health Regions.
</p>

<p style="font-size: 1.05em; color: #334155; margin-bottom: 1em; font-style: italic; line-height: 1.8;">
These predictions will cover three major respiratory viruses:
</p>

<ul style="font-size: 1.05em; color: #334155; margin-bottom: 1.5em; font-style: italic; line-height: 1.8; padding-left: 2em;">
  <li><strong>COVID-19 Hospital Bed Occupancy Count</strong> (wk inc covid hosp)</li>
  <li><strong>Influenza Hospital Bed Occupancy Count</strong> (wk inc flu hosp)</li>
  <li><strong>RSV Hospital Bed Occupancy Count</strong> (wk inc rsv hosp)</li>
</ul>

<p style="font-size: 1.05em; color: #334155; margin-bottom: 1.5em; font-style: italic; line-height: 1.8;">
Predictions will be compared against confirmed hospital bed occupancy data released by <strong>Public Health Ontario's Ontario Respiratory Virus Tool</strong>. This challenge provides an opportunity to contribute to real-time decision-making and resource allocation for healthcare systems during disease outbreaks.
</p>
