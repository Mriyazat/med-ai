Getting Here:

The University of Guelph is located at 50 Stone Road East, Guelph, Ontario. The Albert A. Thornbrough Building is situated in the Engineering precinct of the campus.

  • By Car: Parking is available in various lots across campus. Visitor parking permits can be obtained from Parking Services.
  • By Public Transit: Guelph Transit provides bus service to the university. Check Guelph Transit for routes and schedules.
  • Campus Map: View the University of Guelph campus map for building locations and directions.
