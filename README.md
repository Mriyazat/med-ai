# AI-Driven Medical Imaging & Diagnostics Lab

Official website for the **AI-Driven Medical Imaging & Diagnostics Lab** at the University of Guelph.

The AI-Driven Medical Imaging & Diagnostics Lab focuses on developing cutting-edge artificial intelligence and machine learning techniques to address critical challenges in medical imaging. We are part of the [School of Engineering](https://www.uoguelph.ca/engineering/) at the [University of Guelph](https://www.uoguelph.ca/), Guelph, Ontario, Canada.
